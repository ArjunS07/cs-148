CS148a **Bonus Assignment**
In this bonus assignment, we are interested in comparing our CNN, ViT, and foundation model
checkpoints. We want you to explore how different aspects of training and inference influenced
downstream performance.
This is purely a bonus, so have fun! If something is unclear (e.g. you are not sure if you should
choose a CPU or GPU for inference. You are not sure if you can reuse certain checkpoints you didn’t

are not CNNs or ViTs, how you should make your scatterplots ... etc.), just use your best judgement
and clarify what you did.
Task Overview.

How did the number of total training samples change the validation and training error?

How does the error scale with the number of parameters?

How did the error scale with inference time? Use the same compute resource for all checkpoints,
and include it in your report.
